About this Quiz

This Quiz app is designed to help you prepare for the IELTS (International English Language Testing System) exam. It provides a series of Seventy(70) practice questions similar to those you will encounter in the actual IELTS test, helping you to improve your English language skills and get ready for the exam. Each quiz is carefully crafted to cover different topics, and you can track your performance as you go. Whether you are preparing for the reading, writing, or spell check section, this quiz app will help you sharpen your skills and boost your confidence.
